HOW TO MAKE A CHANGE

It has never been a time more urgent than it is now to start bringing change to this country! If you are just as committed to the empowerment of our Youth as we are, you contributions will go a long way in helping us to reach more South Africans even faster. There is no shortage of people and organisations on our waiting list to roll out our program to, where we can make a real difference in the quality of so many lives on a massive scale.

There are two ways in which you can assist:

  • By donating any amount of money that you are willing to provide.
  • By providing full sponsorships for a candidate or a group of candidates.

Sponsorships may be made in either an individual or corporate capacity. You will be provided with an official invoice to submit for tax-purposes. And because we believe in forging lifelong partnerships with our sponsors, you will receive regular updates on how your funds were allocated as well as on the progress of the candidates that have been sponsored.
